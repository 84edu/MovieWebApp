About MovieWebApp

Welcome to MovieWebApp, your personal cinema companion. This platform was designed to bridge the gap between your love for movies and organized data management.

The Vision

To provide a simple yet powerful interface where users can manage their movie collections and instantly get details about any film in history.

The Tech Stack

Built with Python & Flask, using SQLAlchemy for the database and OMDb API for real-time movie data.

How it works

When you add a movie, the app communicates with a global movie database, fetches the director, release year, and poster, and stores it specifically for your user profile.
